SPHS   At   A   Glance

Shambhupura High School is one of the most reputed schools in Narayanganj District. The school campus is full of scenic beauty and free from din & bustle of the area. The school started it's journey in the middle of 1967.

Apart from sufficient classrooms, the school has one science laboratory and a modern library. The library of this institute is fully organized having more than 800 volumes and internet facilities. The computer lab of this institution is enriched with modern amenities having 26 computers. The institution has its also well-known for running extracurricular activities. It has showed its best with in the short span of time.

Presently, Alhaz Noman Al Alamgir is the Chairman of the Governing Body and Kamruzzaman Jamal is the Headmaster of the school. Currently Headmaster, asistant Headmaster and 20 other Teachers are working here. The classes are being conducted from class VI to class X in Bangla Version.
